Profiler Development

Whilst developing compilers for your custom microprocessor designs, Codeplay feeds back information on optimizations that could be made at the hardware level to increase your processing power and throughput. Codeplay has experience of building optimizing compilers for CPUs, GPUs, DSPs and other custom accelerators, and uses this expertise to advise on how programming models map to your architecture. Performance bottlenecks can be identified via detailed early analysis, saving manufacturers time and money.
