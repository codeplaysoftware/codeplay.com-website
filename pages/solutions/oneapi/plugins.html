Codeplay® oneAPI Plugins for NVIDIA® & AMD

oneAPI is an open, cross-industry and standards-based programming model that delivers a standardized experience across different hardware architectures.

help View Support Packages

Clone or Fork via GitHub.com

Our fully open source plugins for NVIDIA/AMD bring the oneAPI ecosystem to this hardware quickly and easily. You can download the binaries as extensions for Intel®'s oneAPI Base Toolkit, or build the plugins for your own implementation of oneAPI.
