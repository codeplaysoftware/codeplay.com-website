Michael Wong, VP of Research and Development, Charles Macfarlane, VP of Marketing and Rod Burns, Developer Relations Manager are attending this year's AutoSens Conference in Detroit from 14th to 16th May, 2019.

Michael Wong will be presenting “Ensuring safe AI in a car" highlighting the practical engineering challenges of turning deep learning, classical machine vision and sensor fusion algorithms from research prototypes into real-world automotive-grade systems.

Codeplay will be exhibiting the benefits of open standards OpenCL and SYCL on Renesas R-Car V3H platform for rapid development and deployment of ADAS and AV functions.

Codeplay would be delighted to speak to anyone attending the event interested in enabling vehicles with machine learning and vision processing systems. Visit Codeplay’s stand at the event or visit the AutoSens website for more details.
