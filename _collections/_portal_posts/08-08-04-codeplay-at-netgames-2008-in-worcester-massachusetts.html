At the Seventh Annual Workshop on Network and Systems Support for Games NetGames 2008 in Worcester, Massachusetts in October, Codeplay Software and ITI Techmedia will reveal new technology for online games development.  A research paper, "Facilitating Online Development With a Novel Network Scripting Language", has been accepted for presentation at the event, and for publication by ACM press.  The research described in the paper was carried out by Codeplay Software and on behalf of ITI Techmedia as part of its Online Games Development R&D Programme.
