Two of Codeplay’s Research Engineers will attend the 5th Multicore Challenge, which will take place at the University of the West of England, Bristol, UK on the 23rd of September.

Head of Research, Dr. Paul Keir, and Games Technology Programmer, Luke Iwanski, will present Codeplay’s prototype implementation of the upcoming SYCL™ for OpenCL™ specification. During the session we will walk through a number of small SYCL programs and features. For more information see our page on the Multicore Challenge website. If you are curious about what SYCL is, or you‘re interested in GPGPU and heterogenous programming, please stop by our demo pod. See you there!
