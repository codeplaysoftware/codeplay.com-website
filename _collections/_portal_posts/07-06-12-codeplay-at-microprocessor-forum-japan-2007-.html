Codeplay will be at at the Microprocessor Forum in Tokyo on June 19-20, where we will be demonstrating the Sieve C++ Multicore Programming System.

Codeplay will show Sieve-enabled Fractal and Image Generators on x86 PC and PS3. For the gamers present, we'll show the latest version of Cell Factor running with the Ageia PhysX card - it's stunning and our compiler helped!
