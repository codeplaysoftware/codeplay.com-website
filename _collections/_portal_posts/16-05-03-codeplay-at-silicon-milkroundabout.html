Codeplay will be attending the Silicon Milkroundabout recruitment fair in London on 7 - 8 May.

Silicon Milkroundabout is an expansive tech job fair, for developers, designers, product experts, tech marketers & data scientists. Codeplay will join 175+ of the most exciting startups and high-growth tech companies to meet face-to-face with prospective candidates.

Attending from Codeplay will be two of our engineers, Duncan McBain and Mark Miller. We’re always delighted to speak with anyone interested in what we’re doing, so please come and say "hello". If you would like to arrange a meeting with us at the event, please follow us on @codeplaysoft and make contact!
