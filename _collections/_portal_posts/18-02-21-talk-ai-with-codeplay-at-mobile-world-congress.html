Mobile World Congress (MWC) is still growing, and with over 100,000 attendees it literally takes over Barcelona each February. Charles Macfarlane from Codeplay is attending and is looking to discuss opportunities that will enable the latest processors to connect with Artificial Intelligence solutions using open standards. MWC exhibits many of the relevant markets that can benefit from Codeplay’s software products such as mobile devices, intelligent home solutions and automotive.

If you'd like to see a demo of our products and find out how we are enabling Artificial Intelligence on a range of hardware contact Charles Macfarlane. He will be able to explain the benefits of open standards and how Codeplay’s products are solving many of the go-to-market obstacles.

We are always delighted to speak with anyone attending the event who is interested in what we are doing, so if you are attending, please come and say “hello”. If you are interested and would like to arrange a meeting with us at the event, please use our contact form or follow us on @codeplaysoft and make contact!
