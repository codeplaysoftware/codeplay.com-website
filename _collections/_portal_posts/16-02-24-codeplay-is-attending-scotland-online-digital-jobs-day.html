Codeplay will be attending Scotland Online Digital Jobs Day online on Wednesday 9 March.

Our own engineers, Luke Iwanski and Ruyman Reyes, will be representing Codeplay as part of 10 great Scottish companies who are taking part in the Jobs Day. Participating employers have a wide range of exciting opportunities for enthusiastic graduates and experienced professionals. We will be online to chat about upcoming opportunities with Codeplay, as well as information for those considering living and working in Scotland - from how the sector is growing and the thriving tech community, to what it is like living in the cities and countryside!

We’re always delighted to speak with anyone interested in what we’re doing, so please come and say “hello”. If you would like to arrange a meeting with us at the event, please use our contact form or follow us on @codeplaysoft and make contact!
