Andrews Richards, CEO and Uwe Dolinsky, CTO are delighted to be attending the University of Edinburgh Science and Engineering Showcase Event & Dinner which will be held on Tuesday 31st January 2017, Surgeon Hall, Edinburgh. The event and dinner will showcase and celebrate a year of excellent science and engineering at the University and honour the valued support of industry partners. Andrew and Uwe are always delighted to speak with anyone attending the event who is interested in what we are doing, so if you are attending, please come and say “hello”.
