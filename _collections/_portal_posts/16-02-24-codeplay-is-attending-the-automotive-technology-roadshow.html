Codeplay will be attending the Automotive Technology Roadshow across various locations between 29 February - 11 March.

Utilising a unique exhibition trailer with up to 24 exhibition stands, companies will showcase their technologies and services on board. Prime sites will be visited to meet with the Engineering, Production, Design and Development Teams who rarely get a chance these days to visit more traditional trade shows. The Automotive Tour runs every March and visits both OEM and Tier 1 organisations across the UK and Germany. Ford, Land Rover, Jaguar, Honda, Visteon, Toyota, BMW, Delphi and others will be in attendance.

Anyone that is interested in meeting us can attend the following stops on the tour, by contacting us using the details below.

  • Jaguar Land Rover (UK) on Monday 29th Feb.
  • Ford (UK) on Friday 4th March
  • Opel (Germany) on Wednesday 9th March

Attending from Codeplay will be our own Product Marketing Consultant, Charles Macfarlane, as well as one of our engineers, Illya Rudkin. We’re always delighted to speak with anyone interested in what we’re doing, so please come and say “hello”. If you would like to arrange a meeting with us at the event, please use our contact form or follow us on @codeplaysoft and make contact!
