Codeplay are delighted to announce that we will be attending this years British Machine Vision Conference (BMVC) from the 4th-7th September at Imperial College London. Attending from Codeplay  will be Luke Iwanski, Senior Software Engineer, AI Acceleration Research & John Lawson Staff Software Engineer, Machine Learning Research. We are always delighted to speak with anyone attending the event who is interested in what we are doing, so if you are attending, please come and say “hello”. If you are interested and would like to arrange a meeting with us at the event, please use our contact form or follow us on @codeplaysoft and make contact!

The British Machine Vision Conference (BMVC) is one of the major international conferences on computer vision and related areas. It is organised by the British Machine Vision Association (BMVA). More information on the conference can be found here - BMVC London'17
