Codeplay is pleased to announce its attendance at this year's ACCU conference, on the 10th and 11th of April. The attendees, Luke Iwanski and Deepak Panickal, have both been working on exciting technologies including heterogeneous debuggers and runtimes. If you are in the area or are attending this years conference, please feel free to get in touch to arrange a meeting with them.
