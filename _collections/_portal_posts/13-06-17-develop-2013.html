Codeplay are pleased to announce that we will attending this years Develop 2013, hosted in sunny Brighton, UK. It's an exciting time of year for the technology and game industries, and with the impending release of the PlayStation 4 and the XBox One there is sure to be a lot of excitement and interesting information flying about. A few members of our team will be coming along to join in the fun, meet new people, listen to talks and look for talented individuals to come and join our team.

If you are in the area or are attending this years Develop, don't forget to get in touch with us and we will try to arrange a meeting.

You can easily get in touch with us using our support page.
