Jakub Chlanda, Senior Software Engineer, Jack Frankland, Software Engineer, Pierre-Andre Saulais, Principal Software Engineer Compilers and Tomas Matheson, Software Engineer will be attending this year's euroLLVM Developers' Meeting in Brussels from 8th to 9th April, 2019.

The LLVM developers' meeting is a bi-annual 2 day gathering of the entire LLVM Project community. The conference is organized by the LLVM Foundation and many volunteers within the LLVM community. Developers and users of LLVM, Clang, and related subprojects will enjoy attending interesting talks, impromptu discussions, and networking with the many members of our community. Whether you are a new to the LLVM project or a long time member, there is something for each attendee.

Codeplay would be delighted to speak to anyone attending the event interested in connecting Artificial Intelligence to Silicon so please track them down via linkedin or using our contact form on Codeplay's website or follow on Twitter @codeplaysoft.
