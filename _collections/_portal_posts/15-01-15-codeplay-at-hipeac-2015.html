Codeplay will be attending the 2015 HiPEAC Conference in Amsterdam, from 19 – 21 January.

The HiPEAC Conference is the premier European forum for experts in computer architecture, programming models, compilers and operating systems for embedded and general-purpose systems. Associated workshops, tutorials, special sessions, several large poster sessions and an industrial exhibition will run in parallel with the conference.

Attending from Codeplay will be Ralph Potter, Alastair Murray and Lukáš Kuklínek, who will each be contributing to the event’s program of talks, workshops and tutorials:

Alastair will be presenting a talk titled “OpenCL SYCL™: Shared-source heterogeneous parallel programming” as part of the Industrial Session beginning at 2pm on Tuesday.

In the PEGPUM workshop, beginning on Wednesday at 12pm, Ralph will present a talk titled “Implementing Khronos SYCL™ for OpenCL,” followed by Lukáš’ talk, “Accelerating Renderscript applications using OpenCL SPIR.”

We’re always delighted to speak with anyone interested in what we’re doing, so please come and say “hello”. If you would like to arrange a meeting with us at the event, please follow us on @codeplaybiz and make contact!
