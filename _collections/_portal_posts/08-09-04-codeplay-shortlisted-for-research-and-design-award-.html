Electronics weekly have announced that Codeplay has been shortlisted in the Elektra 08 Awards. Codeplay entered in the Research and Design Category with it's work on the Sieve C++ Multicore Programming System.

The Elektra Awards takes place Monday 10th November 2008 at the start of Electronica 2008 exhibition in Munich, Germany.
