The AMD™ CodeXL™ project brought a number of capabilities to developers who are developing for AMD GPUs and CPUs. These enable developers to profile their code to visualize performance and understand execution sequences.

We have been working as part of the LPGPU2 project to expand the capabilities of the CodeXL project to support SYCL™ applications, and we've reached a key milestone. The teams at Codeplay are using CodeXL to ease their development and identify ways to improve their code, and soon it will be available to all SYCL developers.

Find out more on the LPGPU2 project website.
