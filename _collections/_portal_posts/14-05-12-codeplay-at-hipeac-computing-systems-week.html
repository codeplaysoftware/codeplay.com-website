Is Current Research on Heterogeneous HPC Platforms inline with Real-world Application needs? Codeplay’s Paul Keir will join a discussion panel in Barcelona next week, to field this and similar questions. The panel forms part of a 2-slot thematic session organised as part of HiPEAC’s Computing Systems Week (CSW). The session is on Thursday 15 May in the morning from 9:00 to 12:30. There will be two presentations in the earlier morning session from 9:00 to 10:30; with the panel session in the later session from 11:00 to 12:30. See http://hipeac.net/add/res/102/467 for further details.

If you would like to arrange a meeting with us at the event, please contact us. Alternatively, look out for the Codeplay logo on t-shirts and hoodies, as we love nothing more than to have a chat with anyone and everyone!
