Codeplay's Founder and CEO Andrew Richards will be participating as a panelist in an upcoming live webinar on multi-core design organised by Embedded Systems Design/Embedded.com. The webinar will focus on the issues involved in multi-core design and takes place on the 18th December at 1900 UTC.

As well as managing Codeplay Andrew is the chief architect of the Codeplay's Sieve C Multicore Programming System and compiler technologies. Alongside other leading industry figures, Andrew will be providing advice on how to design a multi-core system and answering questions live at the end of the session.
