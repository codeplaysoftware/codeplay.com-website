Rod Burns from Codeplay will be presenting at this year's CDT Industrial Engagement Day, on the 19th February, 2019 at the University of Edinburgh.

The engagement event connects PhD students and industry leaders with like interests to discover potential areas of collaboration, particularly via internships and industry projects.

Codeplay will also have a booth where you can come and ask our software engineers about what we do and the opportunities to work at the company.

The first part of this annual engagement event will focus on student and industrial presentations, so as to inform both groups on each other’s research areas. This shall be followed by a lunch and poster session, during which industry partners can interact with individual students to further explore their work. Industry partners attending the event also include Amazon, Cirrus Logic, Cray, Deepmind, Mathworks, Oracle and Xilinx Scotland.

Rod would be delighted to speak to anyone attending the event interested in connecting Artificial Intelligence to Silicon so please track him down via linkedin or using our contact form on Codeplay’s website or follow on Twitter @codeplaysoft
