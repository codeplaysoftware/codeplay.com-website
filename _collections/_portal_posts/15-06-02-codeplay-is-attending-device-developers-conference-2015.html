Codeplay will be attending Device Developers' Conference 2015 in Uphall, Scotland on Thursday 4 June.

The Device Developers' Conference is a chance to learn about the latest development technologies for Embedded Systems and Real-Time Computer Systems from around the world.

The Device Developers' Conference 2015 is a Free one-day event taking place in Reading, Cambridge, Warrington and Uphall during May/June. Each event will offer technical presentations, technical workshops and a vendor exhibition and will be an opportunity to meet and engage with expert engineers from the companies that are developing the next generation of tools and technologies.

Four of Codeplay Software's engineers, Colin Riley, Aidan Dodds, Scott Straughan and Sean Jones will be attending the Uphall event.

We’re always delighted to speak with anyone interested in what we’re doing, so please come and say "hello". If you would like to arrange a meeting with us at the event, please follow us on @codeplaysoft and make contact!
