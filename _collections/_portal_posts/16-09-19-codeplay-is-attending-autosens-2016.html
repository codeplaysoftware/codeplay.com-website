Codeplay will be attending AutoSens 2016, the The Automotive Sensor & Perception Conference, which is being held from 20-22 September in Brussels, Belgium. AutoSens is Europe's biggest ADAS event.

AutoSens connects technologists in all disciplines of vehicle perception to network, collaborate, solve shared challenges and advance ADAS technologies more rapidly. Bringing together engineers from several engineering disciplines including automotive imaging, LiDAR, radar, image processing, computer vision, in-car networking, testing and validation, certification and standards, AutoSens is a collaborative environment geared towards supporting engineering activities.

In addition to Codeplay's sponsoring and exhibiting at the event ,Andrew Richards, Codeplay's CEO & Founder will also be giving a plenary speech : "The necessary open standards enabling vision processing in ADAS".

Andrew will be speaking about :

  • The benefits of Open Standards and relevance for automotive vision processing
  • Bringing OpenCL™ and SYCL™ into the frame
  • Faster development and high performance with Codeplay’s open-source vision library, VisionCpp

Codeplay is also proud to announce the launch of their ComputeCpp Community Edition Beta, enabling Early Access to the SYCL Open Standard for C++ Acceleration.

Also attending from Codeplay will be our VP of Product Marketing, Charles Macfarlane. We’re always delighted to speak with anyone interested in what we’re doing, so if you're you're attending, please come and say “hello”.
