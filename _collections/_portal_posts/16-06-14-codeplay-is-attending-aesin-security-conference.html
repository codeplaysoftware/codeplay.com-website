Codeplay will be attending the AESIN Security Conference in East Kilbride, near Glasgow on June 16th

Automotive Security now has a major cross-industry focus with increasing levels of connectivity and vehicle system complexity providing tougher challenges to secure against hacks and Cybercrime. This Conference, hosted by NXP Semiconductors will provide insight into the latest thinking and experiences from leading Industry players across the supply chain tackling topics such as secure architecture, cryptographic keys / authentication, resilient application interfaces, the role of software and hardware in protection and remote firmware updates over-the-air.

Attending from Codeplay will be Illya Rudkin, Principal Software Engineer : Safety-Critical Software Development Team Lead and Charles Macfarlane : Product Marketing.

We’re always delighted to speak with anyone interested in what we’re doing, so please come and say “hello”. If you would like to arrange a meeting with us at the event, please use our contact form or follow us on @codeplaysoft and make contact!
