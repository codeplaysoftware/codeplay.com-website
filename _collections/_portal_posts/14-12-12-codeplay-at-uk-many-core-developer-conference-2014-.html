Codeplay will be attending the UK Many-Core Developer Conference 2014 in Cambridge on 15th December.

The UK Many-Core Computing Conference is an informal day of talks spanning the whole landscape of accelerated computing and heterogeneous many-core computing. Topics of interest include high-performance computing, embedded and mobile systems, computational science, finance, computer vision and beyond. The goal of the event is to develop and bring together the UK community of developers, both industrial and academic.

Attending from Codeplay will be Ralph Potter, who will also be presenting a talk : “SYCL™: A system which integrates with OpenCL™”. We’re always delighted to speak with anyone interested in what we’re doing, so please come and say “hello”. If you would like to arrange a meeting with us at the event, please follow us on @codeplaybiz and make contact!
