Codeplay will be attending the Showcasing UK Excellence in Autonomous Driving event in Boblingen, Germany on Thursday 1 October.

UK Trade & Investment is hosting a workshop promoting the UK’s autonomous driving expertise to German businesses. Target audience are decision makers from German car manufacturers and key suppliers including autonomous driving experts from VW, Porsche, BMW, Daimler, Opel, Audi, Continental, Bosch, ZF Friedrichshafen. There will be speakers from BMW, BT Automotive, and MIRA.

Selected UK companies will also be given the opportunity to pitch their innovative autonomous driving solutions in front of the German industry. These companies will also have the opportunity to present their solutions in a small exhibition area during the breaks.

Codeplay's Product Marketing Consultant, Charles Macfarlane, will be attending this event.

We’re always delighted to speak with anyone interested in what we’re doing, so please come and say “hello”. If you would like to arrange a meeting with us at the event, please follow us on @codeplaysoft and make contact!
