Codeplay will be attending New Directions in Operating Systems in London on 25th November.

NDOS is exploring a new stage in innovation in operating systems and in virtualization. The conference's full schedule reflects a widespread excitement over containers, distributed operating systems, security, and new languages which are being used: Mobile, the internet of things, and cloud are changing the way operating systems are used.

Attending from Codeplay will be Aaron Dron, Sean Jones and Colin Riddell.

We’re always delighted to speak with anyone interested in what we’re doing, so please come and say "hello". If you would like to arrange a meeting with us at the event, please follow us on @codeplaybiz and make contact!
