Codeplay has demonstrated its Sieve Parallel Processing Compiler running on a PhysX™ accelerator chip at the Spring Processor Forum in San Jose. The Sieve system is able to compile C++ programs to exploit the parallel processing capabilities of AGEIA's PhysX™ physics accelerator chip. The demonstration proves that the Sieve System can easily be ported to and fully exploit the capabilities of unusual and novell processor architectures.
