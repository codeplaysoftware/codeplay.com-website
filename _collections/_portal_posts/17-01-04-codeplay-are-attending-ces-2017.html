Codeplay will be attending CES (Consumer Electronics Show) 2017 which will be held on the 5th-8th January in Las Vegas, NV. Attending from Codeplay will be our VP of Product Marketing, Charles Macfarlane. We’re always delighted to speak with anyone who is interested in what we’re doing, so if you're you're attending, please come and say “hello”. If you would like to arrange a meeting with us at the event, please use our contact form or follow us on @codeplaysoft and make contact!

'CES has been the showcase of consumer technology innovation since 1967. This year - 2017 marks the 50th Birthday of CES. The International CES (Consumer Electronics Show®) is the world's gathering place for all who thrive on the business of consumer technologies. It's where business gets done: on the show floor, in and around our conference program, in impromptu connections and in planned meetings and special events.' More information on CES can be found here: CES 2017
