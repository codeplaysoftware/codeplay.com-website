Charles Macfarlane, VP of Marketing, will be demonstrating how AI application developers can develop or port their advanced ADAS solutions using well established programming methods. Using Renesas R-Car products with Codeplays ComputeSuite, developers and manufacturers can discuss how OpenCL and SYCL open standards can achieve high performance, fast development times, maintainable and safe AI solutions for automotive.

The 2019 IEEE Intelligent Vehicles Symposium (IV19) is a premier annual technical forum bringing together researchers and practitioners from universities, industry, and government agencies worldwide to share and discuss the latest advances in theory and technology related to intelligent vehicles.
