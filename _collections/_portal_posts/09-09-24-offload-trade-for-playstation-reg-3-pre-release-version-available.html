Codeplay have released an early preview version of Offload for PlayStation®3. This version is available for download immediately for registered PlayStation®3 developers.

The Linux version of Offload, for use on other devices powered by the Cell Broadband Engine™ is still in the final stages of development and as of yet unavailable to the general programming community. Codeplay haven't given a release date but estimate it should be ready for release by mid October.

In the meantime developers can sign up for a free Offload account to be notified of any news updates regarding Offload's availability.
