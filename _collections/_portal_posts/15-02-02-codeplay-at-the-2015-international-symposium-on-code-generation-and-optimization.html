Codeplay will be attending the 2015 International Symposium on Code Generation and Optimization in San Francisco, from 7 - 11 February.

The International Symposium on Code Generation and Optimization (CGO) will bring together researchers and practitioners working at the interface of hardware and software on a wide range of optimization and code generation techniques and related issues. The conferences span the spectrum from purely static to fully dynamic approaches, including techniques ranging from pure software-based methods to architectural features and support.

Attending from Codeplay will be Ruyman Reyes and Fraser Cormack. We’re always delighted to speak with anyone interested in what we’re doing, so please come and say "hello". If you would like to arrange a meeting with us at the event, please follow us on @codeplaysoft or @codeplaybiz and make contact!
