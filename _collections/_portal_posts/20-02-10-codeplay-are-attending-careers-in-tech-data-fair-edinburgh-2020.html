Stuart Adams and Thales Sabino, Staff Software Engineers, will be attending Edinburgh University's Careers in Tech & Data Fair from 12.30pm to 4pm, on Wednesday the 12th February.

The Careers in Tech & Data Fair provides a chance to meet over 50 organisations of all sizes offering different ways into the Tech sector. Exhibitors are keen to hire University of Edinburgh students from a variety of different backgrounds and is a great opportunity for students from all subject areas who are interested in what a career in tech could mean for them. Around half of the organisations attending will be recruiting students from any discipline. It offers a chance to challenge your thinking on what a career in tech looks like and aims to showcase a range of opportunities and types of organisation of all sizes that come under the Careers in Tech & Data banner. It is also an easy way to chat to a range of organisations offering work experience and graduate roles related to tech.
