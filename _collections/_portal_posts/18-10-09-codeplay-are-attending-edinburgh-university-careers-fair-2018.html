Codeplay are pleased to announce that Romain Biessy, Software Engineer, Kostas Kyrimis, Software Engineer, Jack Frankland, Software Engineer and Stephen McGroarty, Software Engineer, Compilers, will be attending this year's Edinburgh University's Careers Fair on the 10th October, from 11am to 4.30pm at McEwan Hall, Bristo Square, Edinburgh. We are always delighted to speak to anyone attending the event who is interested in what we are doing, so please come and say “hello” . If you are interested and would like to arrange a meeting with us at the event, please use our contact form on our website or follow us on Twitter @codeplaysoft and make contact!

Why should you attend? This flagship event welcomes 130 exhibitors and attracts over 4000 students across the two days (9th & 10th) The Careers Fair attracts students from all disciplines, so is ideal for raising your organisation’s profile and promoting opportunities. The event attracts final year, penultimate and early year students. It is ideal for uncovering talent for graduate positions, internships and placements.
