Codeplay is attending the BMVC 2016 conference in York, UK, from 19-22 September. The British Machine Vision Conference (BMVC) is one of the major international conferences on computer vision and related areas. It is organised by the British Machine Vision Association (BMVA).

The conference programme will cover many aspects of image processing machine vision: BMVC Detailed Programme.

Codeplay is also proud to announce the launch of their ComputeCpp Community Edition Beta, enabling Early Access to the SYCL Open Standard for C++ Acceleration.

Attending from Codeplay is Luke Iwanski, Staff Software Engineer, Compute & Graphics. We’re always delighted to speak with anyone interested in what we’re doing, so if you're you're attending, please come and say “hello”. If you would like to arrange a meeting with us at the event, please use our contact form or follow us on @codeplaysoft and make contact!
