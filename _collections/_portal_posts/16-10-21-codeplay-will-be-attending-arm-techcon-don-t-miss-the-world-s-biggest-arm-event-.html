Codeplay will be attending the ARM TechCon which will be happening on the 25th-27th October at the Santa Clara Convention Centre. 

'ARM TechCon 2016 is about to deliver its premier embedded systems experience. Join over 4,500 of your peers, including those from Facebook, Tesla, Google, and Texas Instruments. Experience three days of ARM ecosystem education, networking, and sourcing under one roof. Hear from the luminaries shaping your industry, including Raspberry Pi Founder Eben Upton and SoftBank CEO Masayoshi Son'. Full information on the Conference can be found here: ARM TechCon

Codeplay is also proud to announce the launch of their ComputeCpp Community Edition Beta, enabling Early Access to the SYCL Open Standard for C++ Acceleration.

Attending from Codeplay will be our VP of Product Marketing, Charles Macfarlane. We’re always delighted to speak with anyone interested in what we’re doing, so if you're you're attending, please come and say “hello”. If you would like to arrange a meeting with us at the event, please use our Contact Us Form! or follow us on @codeplaysoft and make contact!
