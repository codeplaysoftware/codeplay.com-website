Some of our finest engineers will be attending the Edinburgh Graduate Recruitment Fair, hosted by The University of Edinburgh at Pleasance Sports Hall.

If you’re interested in a career at Codeplay, why not come and meet us on on Tuesday 26th May between 11am-4pm? Our expert engineers are very friendly and happy to talk to anyone about why Codeplay is a great place to work, as well as giving great advice on applying for a job in technology.

If you’d like to know more about careers at Codeplay, check out our careers pages. If you think that you have what it takes to work at Codeplay Towers, you can apply directly from there, and don’t forget: we love to see samples of your code!
