Codeplay will be at the Develop Conference 2011 in Brighton from 19.-21. July. Please get in touch to arrange a meeting. Codeplay will be talking about the future of GPU programming, in particular running C++ code on GPUs.

Andrew Richards of Codeplay will also be on the Panel discussing future directions in games technology.
