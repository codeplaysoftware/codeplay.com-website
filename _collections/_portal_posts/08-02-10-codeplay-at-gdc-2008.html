Codeplay will be attending the 2008 Games Developers Conference in San Francisco where founder and CEO Andrew Richards will will be available to demonstrate the latest functionality of the Sieve System. He will also be demonstrating an exciting new network scripting language technology developed by Codeplay.

If you would like to meet with Andrew for a demonstration then you can email him to schedule a meeting.
