Codeplay are pleased to announce their membership of the Embedded Vision Alliance (EVA) where Charles Macfarlane, VP Product Marketing will be attending the quarterly members meeting on the 22nd February, in Santa Clara, USA. Whilst at the meeting Charles will be giving a talk introducing other members to Codeplay. 

We are always delighted to speak with anyone attending the members meeting who is interested in what we are doing. If you are attending, please come and say “hello”. If you are interested and would like to arrange a meeting with us at the event, please use our contact form or follow us on @codeplaysoft and make contact!

The EVA members meeting provides opportunity to members to: 

  • Gain an insight into the embedded vision markets, technologies and application
  • Learn about opportunities to grow business through alliance programs, such as the upcoming EVS (Embedded Vision Summit)
  • Meet other representatives of other member companies and give others the chance to learn about the newest alliance member companies and help to identify prospective partners
  • Give Codeplay the opportunity to contribute ideas and priorities to the alliance planning

Codeplay is also proud to announce the new release of ComputeCpp CE, enabling, Early Access to the SYCL Open Standard for C++ Acceleration
