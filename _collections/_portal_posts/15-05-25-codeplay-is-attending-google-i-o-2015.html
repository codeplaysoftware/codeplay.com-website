Codeplay will be attending Google I/O in San Francisco on 28-29 May.

Google I/O is two days of talks, hands-on learning, and a chance to hear more about Google’s latest developer products. The event is aimed at developers and creative coders who are building what’s next.

Two of Codeplay Software’s engineers, Verena Beckham and Meenakshi Ravindran, will be attending the event.

We’re always delighted to speak with anyone interested in what we’re doing, so please come and say “hello”. If you would like to arrange a meeting with Verena and Meenakshi at the event, please follow us on @codeplaysoft and make contact!
