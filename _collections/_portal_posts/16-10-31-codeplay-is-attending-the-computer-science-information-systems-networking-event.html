On Wednesday 2nd November 2016, Codeplay will be attending the specially organised Career Fair at Heriot-Watt University. You will be able to locate Codeplay in the Cedar Suite, Hugh Nisbet Building from 14.30pm - 16.30pm.

'This event has been specifically organised for Computer Science and Information Systems students to raise the level of awareness about the opportunities available within the industry and give an insight into different career areas. It also provides the opportunity to network with potential employers and / or placement providers, as we will put attendees into small groups to take part in "speed networking" with the providers. More information on the Career Fair can be found here: Computer Science and Information Systems Networking Event'

Lots of hiring going on at Codeplay due to new projects coming on stream in a range of high tech new areas: machine learning, C++, machine vision, compilers, programming models and parallel programming we need to hire more smart people to join us to work on these exciting new technologies. Please find attached the link to the Codeplay Careers page if you'd like to apply for any positions we have advertised. 

 Our colleagues, Simon Brand and Ewan Crawford will be there to tell you all about Codeplay and what we do as well as answer your questions about working with us! Pop along and ask us some questions!

We look forward to meeting with you!!
