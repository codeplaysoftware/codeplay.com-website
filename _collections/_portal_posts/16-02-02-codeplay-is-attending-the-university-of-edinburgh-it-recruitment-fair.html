Codeplay will be attending the University of Edinburgh IT Recruitment Fair at the Informatics Forum, 10 Crichton Street, on Wednesday 3rd February.

If you’re interested in a career at Codeplay, why not come and meet us between 1-4pm? Our expert engineers are very friendly and happy to talk to anyone about why Codeplay is a great place to work, as well as giving great advice on applying for a job in technology. We are currently have internships and placements available, as well as permanent positions.

Attending from Codeplay will be Duncan McBain, Simon Brand, Ewan Crawford, and Andrzej Warzynski. We’re always delighted to speak with anyone interested in what we’re doing, so please come and say "hello". If you would like to arrange a meeting with us at the event, please use our contact form or follow us on @codeplaysoft and make contact!
