Codeplay is pleased to announce its attendance at the High-Performance Graphics 2014 conference and the 25th Eurographics Symposium on Rendering, both of which are taking place next week in Lyon. Luke Iwanski and Ralph Potter have both been conducting research on using heterogeneous systems to provide hardware acceleration of graphics algorithms, and look forward to the opportunity to talk to you about heterogeneous computing and high performance graphics.

If you would like to arrange a meeting with us at the event, please email us at infocodeplay.com. Alternatively, look out for the Codeplay logo on t-shirts and hoodies, as we love nothing more than to have a chat with anyone and everyone!
