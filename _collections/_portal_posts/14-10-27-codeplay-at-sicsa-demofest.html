SICSA, the Scottish Informatics & Computer Science Alliance, aims to develop Scotland's position as a world leader in informatics and computer science. They organize an annual DEMOfest event to provide an avenue for academics and industrial partners to find opportunities for collaborative innovation.

Two of Codeplay's research engineers, Dr. Paul Keir and Dr. Alastair Murray, will be attending the next DEMOfest at the University of Edinburgh Informatics Forum on October 30th 2014.

Leading informatics and computer science researchers from various Scottish universities will be showcasing their work, covering a broad range of topics. Paul and Alastair are looking forward to finding out about the great research being done, so if you are attending look out for them and say "Hi"!

We hope to see you there!
