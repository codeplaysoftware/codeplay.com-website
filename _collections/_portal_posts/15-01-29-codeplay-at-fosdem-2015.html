Codeplay will be attending the 2015 FOSDEM conference in Brussels, from 31 January - 1 February.

FOSDEM is a two-day event organised by volunteers to promote the widespread use of open-source software. This edition features 526 speakers, 551 events, and 46 tracks.

Attending from Codeplay will be Pierre-André Saulais. We’re always delighted to speak with anyone interested in what we’re doing, so please come and say "hello". If you would like to arrange a meeting with us at the event, please follow us on @codeplaysoft or @codeplaybiz and make contact!
