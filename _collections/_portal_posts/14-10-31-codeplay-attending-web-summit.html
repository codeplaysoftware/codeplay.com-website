Codeplay will be attending Web Summit (formerly The Summit) in Dublin from 4th - 7th November. Web Summit is a technology conference designed to bring speakers and attendees from some of the largest tech. companies together with some of the newest start-ups. The summit addresses many things from marketing to IoT and Enterprise.

"Web Summit is branching out to cater for the core sectors of business and technology that are making a difference. Builders Summit is one of the cornerstones of this year’s event that is wholly dedicated to the people and companies behind developing the technology that is fundamentally changing the way we live.

With its own campus, main stage, workshops, exhibitors and networking events, Builders Summit will be a conference that is committed to showcasing developers, coders, creators, companies and startups that are building the latest technology.

As an entity within Web Summit, the event will host some of the biggest names in software development, database technologies, interaction design and user experience." (http://websummit.net/summits/builders-summit)

Attending from Codeplay will be Colin Riddell. We’re always delighted to speak with anyone interested in what we’re doing, so please come and say "hello". If you would like to arrange a meeting with us at the event, please use our contact form or tweet @codeplaybiz.
