Codeplay will be attending the University of Glasgow Engineering & Technology Fair on Wednesday 27 October.

This fair will be jam-packed with organisations looking to recruit. If you’re interested in working in either Engineering or Technology this event cannot be missed. Meet with some our engineers and hear first-hand about the opportunities at Codeplay Software.

Several of Codeplay's engineers, Andrzej Warzynski, Simon Brand, Sean Jones, Meenakshi Ravindran, and Deepak Panickal, will be attending this event. We’re always delighted to speak with anyone interested in what we’re doing, so please come and say “hello”. If you would like to arrange a meeting with us at the event, please follow us on @codeplaysoft and make contact!
