Codeplay will be attending the 2016 Embedded Vision Summit in Santa Clara, from 2 - 4 May.

With a focus on deployable computer vision and deep learning, the new expanded program at the Embedded Vision Summit 2016 concentrates your learning and networking experience across three focused days. It opens with a day dedicated to deep learning, the most talked about technique in computer vision today. Day two provides an unparalleled breadth of expert presentations on practical technologies, techniques and opportunities. Day three provides in-depth sponsored workshops on enabling technologies.

The Vision Technology Showcase rounds out the event, featuring demos and exhibits from the leading suppliers in computer vision. Events are presented by industry innovators, leading technologists, and top designers in the field; the provocative, practical sessions are designed for the stakeholders seeking technical education and business opportunities in computer vision.

Attending from Codeplay will be one of our Engineers focusing in this area, Luke Iwanski, as well as our Product Marketing Consultant, Charles Macfarlane. We’re always delighted to speak with anyone interested in what we’re doing, so please come and say “hello”. If you would like to arrange a meeting with us at the event, please use our contact form or follow us on @codeplaysoft and make contact!
