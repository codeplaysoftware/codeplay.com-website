Codeplay will be at at the Microprocessor Forum in San Jose on May 21-23, where we will be demonstrating the Sieve C Multicore Programming System.

Codeplay's technology demonstrations will include Sieve-enabled Fractal and Image Generators on an Intel quad-core x86, on Cell and on a dual-core x86 with the Ageia PhysX card.
