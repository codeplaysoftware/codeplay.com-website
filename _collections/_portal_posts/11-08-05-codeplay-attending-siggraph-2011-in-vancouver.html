Codeplay, the internationally recognized experts in high performance C++ compiler technologies, compilers for programmable GPUs and mobile GPUs, will be demonstrating C++ working on an OpenCL GPU at SIGGRAPH 2011 in Vancouver from August 9-11th (Booth 766).

Please come visit our booth to find out how SoC designers and leading game developers are using Codeplay’s expertise in programmable GPUs and heterogeneous computing and our compiler technology and testing tools to stay ahead of the field in this fast-moving, increasingly mobile world, or contact us beforehand so we can arrange a meeting.

See you there!
