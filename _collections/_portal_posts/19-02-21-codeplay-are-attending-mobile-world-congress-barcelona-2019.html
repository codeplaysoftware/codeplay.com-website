Charles Macfarlane, VP of Marketing will be attending this year's Mobile World Congress event in Barcelona from 25th to the 28th February, 2019.

MWC Barcelona is the largest mobile event in the world, bringing together the latest innovations and leading-edge technology from more than 2,400 leading companies, with a highly-rated conference programme assembling today’s visionaries to explore the hottest topics influencing the industry. MWC Barcelona features extensive learning opportunities from dozens of partner-led programmes, GSMA seminars, summits and more. Everything you need to know about the industry, today and beyond, can be found here. With over 107,000 key decision-makers expected to attend, #MWC19 promises to expand your professional network and help you achieve your goals.

Charles would be delighted to speak to anyone attending the event interested in connecting Artificial Intelligence to Silicon so please track them down via linkedin or using our contact form on Codeplay’s website or follow on Twitter @codeplaysoft
