A new paper, "Strict and Relaxed Sieving for Multi-Core Programming", has just been accepted for presentation at the First Workshop on Programmability Issues for Multi-Core Computers (MULTIPROG 2008), held in conjunction with the 3rd International Conference on High-Performance Embedded Architectures and Compilers (HiPEAC 2008).

A pre-print of the paper will be available online soon.
