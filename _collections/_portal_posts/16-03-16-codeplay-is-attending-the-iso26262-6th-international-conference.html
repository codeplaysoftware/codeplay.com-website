Codeplay will be attending the 2016 ISO26262 6th International Conference in Berlin from 21 - 24 March.

As the biggest and longest running expert forum focused on the technical implementation of ISO 26262, the 6th international conference will include 4 days of interactive sessions and case studies from leading practitioners, best practice methodologies and analysis of real life situations, the latest insights from international ISO working group members, solutions for reducing development costs and improving quality, and discussion of certification procedures.

Attending from Codeplay will be one of our engineers, Illya Rudkin. We’re always delighted to speak with anyone interested in what we’re doing, so please come and say “hello”. If you would like to arrange a meeting with us at the event, please use our contact form or follow us on @codeplaysoft and make contact!
