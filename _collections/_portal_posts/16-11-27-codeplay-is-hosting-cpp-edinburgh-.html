Codeplay is delighted to be hosting the next meeting of C++Edinburgh!

Please come along to the next C++ Edinburgh at 7pm on Monday 28th November! at the Codeplay offices on Level C, Argyle House, 3 Lady Lawson Street, Edinburgh, EH3 9DR.

Two wonderful volunteers have offered to do some interesting talks:

    Creating an entity-component architecture for 3D visualisation in Qt — James Turner

    Minimal RPC framework with Modern C++ — Rui Figueira


As usual, please invite your friends and colleagues along!

For more info, take a look at the website, and don't forget you can follow us on Twitter or Google+.

Any questions, please feel free to get in touch!

C++ Edinburgh is organised by Joseph Mansfield. If you would like to help with the event (perhaps as an organiser, speaker, or sponsor), please get in touch via email at sftrabbitgmail.com or Twitter.
