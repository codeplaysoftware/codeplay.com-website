Calling all students...... On Wednesday 1st February 2017 Codeplay will be attending 'Careers in IT' Recruitment Fair being held at Edinburgh University Informatics Forum between 13.00pm - 16.00pm. We need to hire more smart people to join us to work on these exciting new technologies!!
