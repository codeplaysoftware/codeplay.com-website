Codeplay is pleased to announce that our engineers will be attending the 2014 IEEE High Performance Extreme Computing (HPEC) conference.

HPEC is the largest computing conference in New England and is the premier conference in the world on the convergence of High Performance and Embedded Computing. It is a great opportunity for meeting up with, and learning from, those interested in computing hardware, software, systems, and applications where performance matters.

Our R&D Product Development Lead, Maria Rovatsou, will be presenting a talk on SYCL™: A System which integrates C++ with OpenCL™.

We’re always delighted to speak with anyone interested in what we’re doing, so please come and say hello! Alternatively, if you would like to arrange a meeting with us at the event, please contact us.
