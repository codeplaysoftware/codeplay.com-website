Representatives from Codeplay will be visiting Osaka and Tokyo as part of a UKTI Innovative Technology Mission to Japan. Codeplay is one of 12 companies selected from hundreds of applicant's to take part in this prestigious Trade Mission.

The mission takes place from 8th to 12th September.
