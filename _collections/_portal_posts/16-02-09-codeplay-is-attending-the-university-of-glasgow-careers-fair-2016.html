Codeplay will be attending the University of Glasgow Careers Fair 2016 on Wednesday 10th February.

The Careers Fair, for students and graduates in Scotland, is the umbrella event of the University of Glasgow's career fairs, the perfect place find out about current vacancies; graduate, internship, volunteering and further study.

If you’re interested in a career at Codeplay, why not come and meet us between 11am-4pm? Our expert engineers are very friendly and happy to talk to anyone about why Codeplay is a great place to work, as well as giving great advice on applying for a job in technology. We are currently have internships and placements available, as well as permanent positions.

Attending from Codeplay will be Alistair Low, Sean Jones, and Andrzej Warzynski. We’re always delighted to speak with anyone interested in what we’re doing, so please come and say "hello". If you would like to arrange a meeting with us at the event, please use our contact form or follow us on @codeplaysoft and make contact!
