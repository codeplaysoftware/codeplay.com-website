Codeplay will be attending the FOSDEM 2016 conference in Brussels, from 30 - 31 January.

FOSDEM is a two-day event organised by volunteers to promote the widespread use of open-source software. This edition features 569 speakers, 617 events, and 50 tracks.

Attending from Codeplay will be Duncan McBain and Sean Jones. We’re always delighted to speak with anyone interested in what we’re doing, so please come and say "hello". If you would like to arrange a meeting with us at the event, please use our contact form or follow us on @codeplaysoft and make contact!
