--- title: "Codeplay Software Achieves ISO 9001:2015 Certification" date: 2022-06-30T09:33:10.738000+00:00 layout: portal/portal-article-view user_id: 9001 category: news thumbnail: /assets/images/portal/article-images/iso-celebration.jpg ---

Codeplay Software is proud to have been awarded certification to ISO 9001:2015, an internationally recognized standard for Quality.

Codeplay’s Quality Management System (QMS), with a scope of “Research, Development, Consulting and Technical Support for Software Products and Services on Heterogeneous Systems”, has been rigorously assessed through a two-stage process by BSI Group, one of the world's largest certification bodies, and received exceptional feedback.

"This certification is the result of teamwork initiated by top management. It reflects our commitment to quality products and services as part of our vision for enabling AI & HPC to be open, safe & accessible to all," says Andrew Richards, CEO of Codeplay Software.

Codeplay’s Quality Policy and BSI ISO 9001 certificate can be viewed on our website.
