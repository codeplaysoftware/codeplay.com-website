Three of our finest engineers will be attending the Careers Fair at The University of Edinburgh’s Adam House on Wednesday the 8th of October. If you’re interested in a career at Codeplay, why not come and meet us on Wednesday, between 1:30-6:30pm? Our expert engineers are very friendly and happy to talk to anyone about why Codeplay is a great place to work, as well as giving great advice on applying for jobs with technology companies in general.

If you’d like to know more about careers at Codeplay, check out our jobs pages. If you think that you have what it takes to work at Codeplay Towers, you can apply directly from there, and don’t forget: we love to see samples of your code!
