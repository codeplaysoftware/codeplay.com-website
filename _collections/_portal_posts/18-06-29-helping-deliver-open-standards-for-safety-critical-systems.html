In April Khronos introduced the Khronos Safety Critical Advisory Forum, where members come together to bring functional safety to open standards. There is an increasing interest in using OpenCL and SYCL in the automotive sector, and Codeplay are at the forefront of ensuring that implementations of these standards can be used in safety critical systems such as cars.
My colleague Illya has written a guest post on the Khronos blog here that explains what the group will deliver, and outlines why you should lend your expertise by joining the forum to deliver Khronos open standards for safety critical systems.
