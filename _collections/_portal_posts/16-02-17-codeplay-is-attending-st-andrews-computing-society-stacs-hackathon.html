Codeplay will be attending the St. Andrews Computing Society (STACS) Hackathon, from 20 - 21 February.

The St Andrews Computing Society connects students who share an interest in computing and technology. They hold social events that empower hackers to meet new people and learn new skills.

The Hackathon gathers groups of participants to create unique and innovative projects in just 24 hours - a "jam session for makers".

Attending from Codeplay will be Simon Brand and Peter Carragher. We’re always delighted to speak with anyone interested in what we’re doing, so please come and say "hello". If you would like to arrange a meeting with us at the event, please use our contact form or follow us on @codeplaysoft and make contact!
