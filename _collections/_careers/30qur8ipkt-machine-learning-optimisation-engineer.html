Codeplay is working closely with a prestigious semiconductor vendor to enable and accelerate machine learning models on its high-performance specialised AI hardware, using an open-source AI graph compiler. Codeplay's work encompasses improvements at the graph compiler level to support new models, and performance optimizations to the compiler and directly to the micro-kernels that implement the nodes. Examples of typical optimizations include: vectorization, splitting operations across accelerator devices, reordering and combining operations to reduce data movement and computation, and using target-specific intrinsics to target custom processing units.

As the compiler and kernels are so intrinsically linked, an ideal candidate would be comfortable switching between roles and tackling problems wherever makes most sense.

This project involves working closely with engineers from the customer's large team and an existing successful team within Codeplay, providing opportunities to learn from an excellent and very experienced community of engineers. Your work will contribute to achieving benchmark results that are published publicly and shared with big customers, so this is an ideal opportunity for someone with a passion for making things go fast!

As a Software Engineer in the Customer Engineering division, you will be participating in projects delivering our software platform to internal and external customers. Projects and customers can vary over the years, and there are plenty of opportunities to learn new technologies and jump into different projects upon finishing one. There is also an internal recruitment process that allows moving across to other divisions to work in other areas of the company, such as Product Engineering, Ecosystem Development, Research, or Safety.
