--- id: 28 category: offload-library date: 2020-07-07T17:23:33.7200Z date_modified: 1970-01-01T01:00:00.3600Z title: "-memcpydmasize=[n]" redirect_from: - /kb/28 layout: products/offload/kb/view ---

Sets the default size of the temporary buffer that unaligned DMAs use to bring data local (defaults to 1024). Sizes above 16384 bytes will be floored to 16384.
